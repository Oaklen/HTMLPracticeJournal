Garlic Butter Pasta

Garlic Butter Pasta is a quick and comforting dish made with tender noodles tossed in a rich, savory garlic butter sauce. It's a simple yet flavorful meal that's perfect on its own or paired with your favorite protein or veggies.

Ingredients

  • 200g (about 7 oz) spaghetti or your favorite pasta
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: grated Parmesan, chopped parsley, red pepper flakes

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Reserve ½ cup of pasta water, then drain.
  2. In a large pan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and sauté for 1 to 2 minutes until fragrant (dont let it brown).
  4. Add the drained pasta and toss to coat.
  5. If it seems dry, add a bit of the reserved pasta water.
  6. Season with salt and pepper. Add Parmesan, parsley, or red pepper flakes if you like.
